Trust & integrity

Built so every vote counts, once.

01

One student, one vote

Your matric number and department email are checked against the departmental register before you can vote — and locked once you've cast your ballot.

02

Encrypted in transit

Your selections are encrypted before they leave your browser, so nothing readable ever crosses the network.

03

Anonymous by design

Once cast, your vote ID is separated from your identity. Not even administrators can match a ballot back to a voter.

04

Independent audit trail

Every vote is logged immutably, so results can be verified after the election closes without exposing any single voter's choice.
